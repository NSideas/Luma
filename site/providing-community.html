Past events

August 9, 2016

Steel Magnolias at the Cleveland Play House

Are you a Truvy, M’Lynn, Ousier or Shelby? Our guests were treated to tickets to see Steel Magnolias and learned which Steel Magnolia they are at a pre-show reception with Cleveland Play House artistic director, Laura Kepley.

April 30, 2016

The Patty Wood Girls Leadership Academy

We visited the First Tee of Cleveland to learn about The Patty Wood Girls Leadership Academy, a unique program for girls age 12 – 14. The PWGLA mentors (past participants) shared how the program has helped them stand up and speak up as leaders. Then we joined the young leaders for yoga!

January 26, 2016

Investing in Women with Pax Ellevate

Two Investment News “Women to Watch,” including Luma Wealth’s Heather Ettinger, teamed up in Cleveland to discuss gender diversity, women’s leadership and why investing in women is smart business. Our guests joined them in a roundtable discussion on how to help advance women into leadership roles.

August 10, 2015

Madonna as Icon

The Rock and Roll Hall of Fame hosted a group of our guests of all ages to discuss the one and only Queen of Pop, Madonna! Afterwards, they were treated to a private viewing of the Herb Ritts special photography exhibit.
